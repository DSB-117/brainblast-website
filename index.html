What Brainblast is today

A proof-gated immune system for SDK mistakes.

Most agent safety tools warn from memory. Brainblast manufactures evidence: the failing code, the fixed code, the regression test, the source trail, and the receipt that the fix actually moved from RED to GREEN. That evidence becomes both trainable data and live protection.

Trust boundaries

Proof enters enforcement. Experience stays advisory.

Brainblast is deliberately local-first. Federation helps teams share experience, but no shared belief enters the corpus or scanner until it is independently re-proven.

Nothing becomes shared memory without proof

Bad guesses do not become everyone’s belief. The corpus requires reproducible RED→GREEN evidence.

Enforcement has zero hosted dependency

The scanner runs offline and loads pinned, mirrored rule packs. Registry sync improves freshness, not basic safety.

Team spaces are capability-based

Spaces use unguessable capability IDs, signed events, rotation, and optional signed ACL policies.
