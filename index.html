Research before a single line is written

Point it at your requirements. brainblast inventories every external component (SDKs, APIs, auth providers, chains) and researches each one from live official sources. Facts are fetched, never recalled. Out comes a cited component map, a risk heatmap, and report.json.

Turn risk into an exit code

A deterministic auditor with 29 checkers verifies the generated code against the research. /brainblast-verify proves each predicted fault is gone, and --ci mode fails the build on unresolved CRITICAL or HIGH risks. No vibes. A gate.

Dependencies drift. The gate reopens.

When an advisory lands or a pinned dependency changes, brainblast re-researches the surface and reopens the gate before the drift becomes an incident. Your research report stays alive as your stack moves.

Every catch makes every agent smarter

Each proven catch becomes a verifiable training instance: RED code, GREEN fix, reproducible proof. The corpus feeds back into the tool, syncs across the HiveMind, and pays its contributors in $BRAIN. A retrieval tool can't copy a proof.
